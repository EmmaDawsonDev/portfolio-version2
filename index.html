About me

Hello! I’m Emma! I am a full stack web developer originally from the UK but now residing in a sleepy suburb of Stockholm, Sweden.

I first learnt about programming from my previous career as a primary school teacher when programming was added to the curriculum. This piqued my curiosity, developed into a hobby and then a passion and eventually led to a career change.

The thing I love most about coding is that there’s always something new to learn so it’s impossible to get bored. I'm passionate about making inclusive and accessible experiences for everyone.
